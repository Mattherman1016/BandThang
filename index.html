matt

Matt was born on the side the road and adopted by a family of mongooses (mongees). All he had was his grit and a deep love for ambient funk. Over the years, he learned how to hunt, gather food and play the altered scale. He developed a deep appreciation for Jerkob Colliflower, Spanky Purpey, and VolfPerks and decided to take on massive debt to persue his passion for music.
